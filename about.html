Brief Introduction

  • The xRNA Laboratory was established in 2024, focusing on cutting-edge exploration in RNA science and technology. The lab's name carries dual meanings: the "x" symbolizes both the spirit of scientific exploration into the unknown (X-factor) and forms a value framework of EXCELLENCE-RESILIENCE-NOVELTY-ASPIRATION, embodying our mission to "decode life's mysteries through RNA and drive human health through innovation."
