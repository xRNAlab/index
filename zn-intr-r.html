R - Resilience

  • Embodies the indispensable spirit of resilience in scientific inquiry. Facing the complexity and variability of experiments, we prioritize cultivating students’ problem-reconstruction abilities and psychological flexibility. Through a "mentor-team-individual" support system, we guide members to explore the unknown in experiments, encouraging them to transform research setbacks into opportunities for cognitive growth and forge a scientific character capable of continuous breakthroughs in uncharted territories.

About X,R,NandA

  • These four pillars form the laboratory’s unique ethos: rooted in excellence, supported by resilience, driven by innovation, and guided by aspiration. We strive to remain at the forefront of RNA science, cultivating a globally competitive team of RNA researchers.
