Brief introduction

  • Xu Congcong, Ph.D., is a Young Distinguished Professor at Soochow University's School of International Innovative Medicine and a Shanghai Magnolia Program Pujiang Talent. He earned a Chemistry B.S. from Hunan University and a Pharmaceutical Sciences Ph.D. from The Ohio State University. From 2020-2023, he led an mRNA platform in a top company. Now at Soochow, he oversees research projects, has co-authored papers in Nature and other top journals, holds two international patents, and focuses on novel RNA vaccines and drugs.
